TABLE LINENS

Looking for table decor that's as memorable as your event? Silk & Willow table linens are naturally plant dyed and made from 100% natural materials. We stock a range of table linens—from napkins with finished or unfinished edges to table runners, sheer fabric overlays, and table covers—that complement a variety of design styles and event themes. Adorn your table with color crafted from the finest quality, sustainably harvested plant materials. By combining different types of flowers, leaves, bark, and roots, we create an array of exclusive, one-of-a-kind hues. Just as nature varies from season to season, our color batches may also have slight variations.
